Surface

Provides components (or any element) with a material mouseover effect.
Directive
mdc-surface
Properties Description
boolean Activate or de-activate mouseover effect. (Default is true)
Card - Touch or Mouse over 
<mdc-card [mdc-surface]="true">
  <mdc-card-primary>
    <mdc-card-title>My card - Click me</mdc-card-title>
    <h2 mdc-card-subtitle>Subtitle</h2>
  </mdc-card-primary>
</mdc-card>
